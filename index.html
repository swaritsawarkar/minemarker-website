( 04 )

The workflow is
dead simple.

01

Start recording + start session

/pixelcut start hardcore-ep-3

Fire up OBS, open your world, and start a PixelCut Pro session.

02

Play and mark moments

Press M — or — /pixelcut mark diamond_ore found near lava

Hit the keybind or type a command. Deaths and events are auto-captured.

03

Stop and export

/pixelcut stop

Session exports to JSON, TXT, CSV — ready for the timeline viewer.

04

Open the viewer and edit

Load Last Session → Click markers → Jump to moments

Visual timeline with your video. Click any marker to jump. Get clip suggestions. Export editing notes.
